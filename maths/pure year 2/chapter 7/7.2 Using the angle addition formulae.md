Example

Show, using the formulae for sin(A-B), that $sin15 = \frac{\sqrt{6} - \sqrt{2}}{4}$

---
$sin15 = sin(45-30)$

$= sin45cos30-cos45sin30$

$= (\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2})(\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}) - (\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2})(\frac{1}{2})$

$= \frac{1}{4}(\sqrt{3}\sqrt{2} - \sqrt{2})$

$= \frac{\sqrt{6}-\sqrt{2}}{4}$
